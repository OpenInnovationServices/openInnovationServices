Cultivate Creative Collaboration and Cocreate Opportunities

Our Innovation Sprints

We imagine Innovation Sprints as high energy, engaging, playful collaborative opportunities for employees to design new, potentially transformative products, services, ways of working and/or business models. 

Our Sprints vary in duration from a half-day to say 20 or 30 hours in total over several weeks. We facilitate in-person, remotely (using our partner’s world-class collaborative platform and our multimedia content) or blended - whichever suits you.

“Fergal unleashed the very best of talent from our young, vibrant team during the Sprint..who got out and talked with our customers, brought fantastic ideas to life, progressed where they were easily implemented to add real commercial value...teams loved impacting on business strategy and were energized by the collaborative cross-functional, customer-centric and inspiring nature of the event.”

Ann-Marie Cregan, Director of Buying & Merchandising

Our ultimate goal is to ensure that your temporary innovation teams deliver outcomes that exceed their and your expectations. Our empathetic facilitators create accessible, customised content and step-by-step coaching feedback (see below) that has been developed, adapted and honed by us over literally hundreds of Sprints.

How Sprints Work

1

THE BIG ISSUES

Management identify product, service, process or system problems or opportunities in your business.

2

YOUR FUTURE INNOVATORS

Teams, remote or in-person, tackle  problems using design thinking,
agile innovation and lean startup.

3

DISCOVER, DEFINE & IDEATE

Teams quickly discover what customers & stakeholders want. Then figure out compelling solutions.

4

THE FUN PART

They move on to rapidly prototype, develop a business model, test & iterate their proposed solutions.

5

YOU'LL NEVER WALK ALONE

Experienced mentors, subject experts & peers push teams to work to timelines that suit your business.

6

PITCH & ASK

Your teams pitch their tested solutions to management & recommend next steps.

The Sprint process they follow at Open is brilliant & proven..a combination of design thinking & business model innovation..they created a safe environment where our cross-functional teams collaborated so effectively to pitch solutions that had real value for our finance shared service business. I couldn't recommend it highly enough.

Wayne Gibbons, Finance Director
