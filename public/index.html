How Innovation Sprints Work

1

THE BIG ISSUES

Management identify product, service, process or system problems or opportunities in your business.

2

YOUR FUTURE INNOVATORS

Teams, remote or in-person, tackle  problems using design thinking,
agile and lean.

3

DISCOVER, DEFINE & IDEATE

Teams quickly discover what customers & stakeholders want. Then figure out compelling solutions.

4

THE FUN PART

They move on to rapidly prototype, develop a business model, test & iterate their proposed solutions.

5

YOU'LL NEVER WALK ALONE

Ideas from your diverse workforce collide to generate fresh valuable solutions for your organisation
